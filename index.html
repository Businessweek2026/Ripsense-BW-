Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions?

How long do the internal optical sensors last before needing replacement?
The advanced infrared spectroscopic sensors are built for high-traffic industrial environments with an operational lifespan of over 50,000 scanning hours — approximately 5–7 years of continuous supermarket use before requiring standard calibration service.
How should the scanning bay be cleaned and maintained?
Because it's a non-contact sensor system, produce never physically touches the lenses. The open rectangular bay uses food-safe, wipe-clean composite material and can be cleaned daily with a dry microfiber cloth or mild sanitizing wipe.
Does the machine require an active internet connection to function?
No. Core ripeness analysis algorithms run locally on the internal Data Processing Unit. An internet connection is only required for initial setup, remote diagnostics, and receiving seasonal software updates for new produce varieties.
Can the RipeSense™ scanner handle multiple types of produce at once?
The scanner is optimized to scan one item at a time to guarantee maximum precision and accuracy. Simply select the item type on the touchscreen, place the fruit, and receive a pinpoint result in under 2 seconds.
